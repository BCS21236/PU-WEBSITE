About Our Certificate Programs

Information Technology is the offshoot of Punjab University College of Information Technology (PUCIT) with the aim to provide a state of the art learning environment to the youth of nation. The history of PUCIT dates back to 1987 when the Department of Computer Science was established at the University of the Punjab, Lahore. The Department of IT offers BS in Information Technology, BS Computer Science and M.Sc in Information Technology Programs.
